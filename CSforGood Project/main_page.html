Effectiveness of New York City Recycling Program Over time - #CSForGood

This project was created to demonstrate the effectiveness of the NYC recycling program over time. It does this by allowing the user to compare recycling data from different districts at different times of the year.

How to use:

To use select the years/months/districts you want to include in your datasets and click "submit." You can also decide whether you want data sorted from high to low, or low to high.

Be aware that each column needs to be submitted separately.
